OUR SERVICES

As a leading IT solutions and system integration company, we offer an extensive array of services designed to enhance your businesst

  • - Private Cloud

    Private Cloud offers hosted IT services, provisioned over private IT infrastructure, for the dedicated use of a single organization. It lies internally, behind the company’s firewall, providing the benefits of a Cloud infrastructure while minimizing the security concerns some organizations have about Cloud, and allowing for direct control over the data.

  • - Network Management

    Networks are crucial to infrastructure, connecting various devices and services. As they expand, issues like bottlenecks, configuration errors, and device failures can cause downtime. Network Management utilizes integrated, automated solutions to ensure availability, performance, and agility, reducing error risk and allowing swift response to emerging issues.

  • - Security and Surveillance

    Our Video Surveillance Solutions offer various IP cameras, including indoor, panoramic, PTZ, and outdoor options for commercial use, as well as thermal and long-range cameras for special applications. We provide high-end, high-quality cameras and cost-effective options with resolutions from 1MP to 48MP.

  • - Structured Cabling

    We offer top-notch network connectivity solutions for data centers, enterprises, and small businesses, including copper and fiber infrastructure systems, wireless enclosures, and intelligent port management. These reliable solutions have been implemented in advanced applications worldwide.

  • - Endpoint Security

    Endpoints, where many user actions occur, are often vulnerable and high-risk. To protect them and their data, it's essential to employ multiple security layers, including Enterprise Anti-Virus/Anti-Spyware, Network Access, Host Intrusion Prevention, and Data Loss Prevention solutions.

  • - UPS Systems

    Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems are essential components of any business or organization's IT infrastructure. They protect critical equipment and systems from power outages, fluctuations, and other power-related issues that could lead to costly downtime, data loss, and hardware damage.

  • - Voice & IP Telephony

    IP Telephony describes a technology that uses the Internet Protocol to provide voice access to end users. IP telephony replaces the traditional Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN). The call travels in the form of packets, over a Local Area Network (LAN), or the Internet, avoiding PSTN tolls.
